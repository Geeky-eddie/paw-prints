Adoption Partners

LA Animal Rescue is a non-profit animal rescue based in the San Fernando Valley. Not breed specific or species specific. Run by volunteers and funded by donations.
We are a 501 c3 Non Profit dedicated to saving animal lives, education and animal welfare. We rely on donations to feed and care for the animals we save.
Kitty Bungalow Charm School for Wayward Cats TNR's feral cats and rescues feral kittens in the Los Angeles area. Founded in the backyard of a South LA home in 2009, the "Old Bungalow" became the Los Angeles hub for rescuing, socialization, and adoption of feral kittens.
Santé D’Or Foundation, a 501(c) 3 not-for-profit (Federal ID Number 68-0547925), is a volunteer run animal adoption center and community focused rescue located in Los Angeles, California. Our animals come to us from our streets, are surrendered by residents experiencing extreme financial hardship, a crisis, or those who are no longer able to care for them
Foxy and the Hounds is a Los Angeles based 501(c)(3) non-profit dog rescue organization. Our mission is to help the ones that need us most! We believe every dog deserves a chance at life and we are committed to saving dogs from euthanasia in our local shelters.
We are a 501c non-profit organization located in the South Bay, working to place abandoned and unwanted dogs in safe and permanent homes.
We are a non-profit 501 (c)(3) Foster & volunteer based, all animal, all breed rescue. Our goal is to rescue animals at risk of being euthanized from shelters, abandoned, abused, and unwanted animals.
Cat Connection is a non-profit 501(c) no-kill cat and dog rescue organization officially established in 1996.
Let Them Be Little works to reduce euthanasia rates by rescuing underage and at-risk cats and kittens and placing them into loving foster homes until they are ready to go to their forever family.
The Animal Rescue Mission is a 501c3 organization that was created to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome animals while educating the public on how to provide a safer, happier world of animals.
Balooja's Foundation is a small Southern California rescue organization run by a group of dedicated volunteers. We save dogs from death at over-crowded shelters, dogs with special medical needs, handicapped dogs, older dogs, and we find them loving, happy homes.
Lionel's Legacy is an approved 501c(3) non-profit organization in the state of CA. We rely solely on generous donations and grants from individuals, businesses, and organizations to fund all of our programs
It’s The Pits is a 501c3 canine rescue that coordinates the fostering and adoption of dogs that haven’t found their permanent home.
The Lovejoy Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, no-kill animal welfare organization dedicated to transforming the lives of animals in need.
Much Love Animal Rescue exists to rescue abused, neglected and homeless animals from the streets and shelters of Los Angeles and place them in loving homes.
Frosted Faces Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non profit organization delivers the promise of family and quality veterinary care for senior animals whose love and lives are in jeopardy.
Our Goal is to assist in the Rescue of Dogs and Cats from High Kill Shelters. We also foster and assist in the networking of pets in need of permanent loving homes.
We are a 501c3 non-profit animal rescue organization serving sunny Los Angeles California! We network and rescue cats and kittens from the high kill L.A.
Karlee's Angels is a 501c3 community led animal rescue whose members have over twenty-five years of experience in animal rescue and reuniting lost pets with their owners.
Wags and Walks is dedicated to rescuing family-friendly dogs from shelters and finding them safe and suitable permanent homes through adoption, advocacy and education.
We are a 501 C3 non-profit no-kill animal rescue organization, located in Murrieta CA We rescue stray dogs and cats from the streets, and from situations of abuse and neglect.
Sierra Pacific Furbabies is a 501c3 animal rescue with locations in both SoCal and NorCal. Our mission is rescue and rehoming of homeless pets.
Maui Cat Rescue’s mission is to facilitate adoptions of homeless cats on Maui and limit overpopulation. Maui Cat Rescue fosters cats, provides them with high level care, and facilitates adoptions through its cat lounge, a warm and inviting space where guests can come to spend time with cats, relax and rejuvenate and potentially adopt.
The Wetlands & Wildlife Care Center is a non-profit 501(c)3 dedicated to the rehabilitation and release of injured and orphaned native wildlife in Orange County.
The Pacific Wildlife Project provides life-saving care to our planet's most vulnerable members
